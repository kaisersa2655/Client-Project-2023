Where do donations go?

Donations are used to fund our events, to throw fundraisers for the community and to find more resources for our exhibits. We are dedicated to giving an affordable place for families to take their children (and themselves!). We also host weekly gaming nights and donations go towards helping us give more opportunites to those who need a fun place to meet people and explore their passions. In the future we are looking to organzie many competititve gaming teams.
